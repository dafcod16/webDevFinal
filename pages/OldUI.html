As the holiday season approaches, everybody is trying to find the best deals around online! I came across one of my favorite websites, Urban Outfitters. I was attempting to buy something for myself for christmas when I came across an unsettling UI detail.


As you can see, this is the checkout page where you'd enter your information for shipping. This page looks excellent until you try to enter/change your location.



Once you click the Country/Region textbox, you're greeted with an incredibly intrusive dropdown list. This is a bad example of UI because it covers up crucial information, takes a lot of scrolling, and can be easily simplfied.
